The right skills for the job

Based out of Vancouver BC, TopoGraphix creates custom maps and graphics for your business needs. We specialize in mineral exploration and environmental data mapping and have the tools and the skills to create the figures you need in a timely and budget-friendly manner. TopoGraphix has a competitive edge in combining technical skills with an eye for design to create maps and graphics that are aesthetically pleasing and convey concise messages to viewers. Over 4 years of experience working in the field of Geographic Information Science (GIS) means that the design process is comprehensive and efficient.

We are:


► Highly responsive

► Attentive to minute details

► Driven by professsional and aesthetic design


Do you have high quality spatial data, yet lack the graphics to tell its story in a meaningful way? Contact TopoGraphix today to see how we can help your business with your mapping and graphics needs!
